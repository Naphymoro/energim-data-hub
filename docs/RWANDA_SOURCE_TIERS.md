# Rwanda Source Tier Framework

## Tier 1 — Official Baseline Authority

Primary institutions:
- MININFRA
- REG
- EDCL
- EUCL
- NISR
- REMA

Primary use:
- historical baseline calibration
- official energy accounting
- national statistics
- LEAP baseline development
- NDC reporting support

## Tier 2 — Official Supporting Sources

Institutions:
- RURA
- MINECOFIN
- official policy documents

Primary use:
- tariffs
- policy assumptions
- financing context
- regulatory information

## Tier 3 — Supplementary Private and Project Sources

Sources:
- EPD
- IPP project reports
- private-sector energy datasets
- clean energy project platforms

Primary use:
- project pipeline intelligence
- technology deployment tracking
- scenario enhancement
- innovation mapping

## Tier 4 — International Reference Sources

Sources:
- World Bank
- IEA
- IRENA
- SEforALL
- UNFCCC

Primary use:
- benchmarking
- proxy estimates
- cross-validation
- regional comparisons

## Tier 5 — Literature and Assumptions

Sources:
- literature estimates
- expert assumptions
- temporary placeholders

Primary use:
- interim modelling assumptions
- gap filling where no validated data exists

## Core Principle

Official baseline datasets should not be overridden by private or proxy datasets unless validated by relevant Rwanda authorities.
