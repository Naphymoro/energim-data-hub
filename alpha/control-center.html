How EPD helps

EPD private-sector and project-level records can identify private generation, mini-grids, technology costs, financing, project status and future pipeline evidence that may be missing from consolidated official datasets.

Governance rule

Official baseline remains controlled by government sources such as REG, MININFRA, NISR, EDCL and EUCL. EPD creates augmentation candidates that require reconciliation before LEAP/NEMO export.
